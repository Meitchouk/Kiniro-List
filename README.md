<h1>Kiniro List</h1>

<p style="font-size: 18px; color: #555; margin: 20px 0;">
    Hello, I'm Kiniro List. I'll help you discover, track, and organize your favorite anime effortlessly.
</p>

<h2>What I can do for you</h2>

<ul style="line-height: 1.8; color: #333;">
    <li><strong>Explore seasons</strong>: Browse current season, upcoming season, and any season from the past</li>
    <li><strong>Search anime</strong>: Find any title you're looking for</li>
    <li><strong>Complete details</strong>: Read detailed information about each series</li>
    <li><strong>Your personal library</strong>: Save your progress with just one login (Google)</li>
    <li><strong>Organize your list</strong>: Mark as watching, planned, completed, paused, or dropped</li>
    <li><strong>Know when it airs</strong>: Get information about when new episodes come out</li>
    <li><strong>It's yours</strong>: Customize language (EN/ES), theme (light/dark), timezone</li>
    <li><strong>Speaks your language</strong>: Full support in English and Spanish</li>
    <li><strong>Fast and lightweight</strong>: Optimized to run without slowdowns</li>
</ul>

<h2>Where I get my information</h2>

<p style="color: #333; line-height: 1.6;">
    This project depends entirely on <a href="https://anilist.co/" target="_blank" style="color: #0066cc; text-decoration: none;"><strong>AniList</strong></a> for all anime information. AniList is an open community platform that maintains one of the most complete and accurate anime databases on the internet.
</p>

<h3>Data we get from AniList:</h3>
<ul style="color: #333;">
    <li>Complete anime information (title, description, genres, status)</li>
    <li>Seasons and weekly releases</li>
    <li>Episode schedules</li>
    <li>Scores and popularity</li>
    <li>And much more</li>
</ul>
